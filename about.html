Hi, we're Nicholas
& Mackenzie.

It started with a Thanksgiving in London in 2021 — Mackenzie's first international trip, Nick's idea of a casual introduction to the world. She showed up with an open mind and zero air miles to her name. He showed up with a spreadsheet.

A few years later: married, temporarily relocated to Texas (we don't talk about it), and now firmly back in the Pacific Northwest where we belong — with a growing list of countries, a strong opinion on what makes a good pastry, and a running tally of trails Mackenzie has confidently navigated us off of.

This is our travel journal. Nothing fancy, no agenda — just honest stories from the road, the trail, and the occasional wrong turn.

How It Started

A Brief, Honest History

We met in early 2020 — peak pandemic, minimal options. Our first dates were hikes, because what else were you going to do. Mackenzie had grown up in the Pacific Northwest and somehow never really taken advantage of it. Nick fixed that, starting with local trails and eventually working his way up to international flights.

The first international trip was Thanksgiving 2021 in London with his family. Mackenzie showed up with no passport stamps and a lot of enthusiasm. She's been hooked ever since — though her enthusiasm for navigation remains, generously speaking, a work in progress.

We got married in June 2022, spent three years in Texas (a chapter we've closed and don't revisit), and moved back to the Pacific Northwest last year. The deal we made with ourselves: actually use it this time. More trails, more non-rainy-day hikes, more of the views that were always right there waiting.

Hiking wasn't always the anchor of our trips — it grew into that. This year Switzerland, the Grand Tetons, and Yosemite are all on the list, all built around trails. We're not serious hikers in any technical sense. We're just two people who discovered that the best way to see a place is usually on foot, and that a pastry at the end of a long day tastes significantly better than one you didn't earn.

This blog is where we write it all down — the trips, the logistics, the food, the wrong turns. Mostly for us, but hopefully useful for you too.
